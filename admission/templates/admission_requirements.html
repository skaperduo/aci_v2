College & Graduate School Admission Requirements

The following procedures shall be observed:

  1. Fill out the Student Admission Form at the Registrar Office.
  2. Submit the accomplished form together with the required documents to the Registrar Office for validation (see below).
  3. New college students and transferees will have to take the ACI Admission Test to assess their aptitude and behavior.
  4. The ACI Admission Test results and admission status will be sent to the applicants when it is made available. Upon acceptance, proceed with the rest of the enrollment process.
  5. Consult the Registrar Officer about your desired program/s to enroll in and the registration schedule.
  6. Pay the corresponding fees to the Cashier.
