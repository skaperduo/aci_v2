College & Graduate School Admission Requirements

The following procedures shall be observed:

    NEW STUDENTS
  1. Students PAYS Examination fee at the Cashier’s Office for Under Grad. only Students goes to the Guidance Office to take the Entrance Exam.
  2. Get a Temporary Enrolment Form from the Registrar's Office.
  3. Student proceeds to Dean Assigned for interview and checking Schedule of Classes.
  4. Student proceeds to the Accounting Office for Computation of Charges.
  5. Student goes to the Cashier’s Office & pays the required down payment.
    *FINAL ENROLMENT FORM ( FEF)
    *STUDENT ID NO.
  6. Student FILLS-UP the FINAL ENROLMENT FORM COMPLETELY. ( Please Print Legibly)
  7. Student proceeds to the REGISTRAR's OFFICE for submission of the following enrolment requirements.
    OLD/TRANSFEREE STUDENTS
  1. Students update their CURRICULUM EVALUATION at the Office of the Dean/Program Head and ask for TEMPORARY ENROLMENT FORM (TEF).
  2. Student proceeds to Dean Assigned for interview and checking Schedule of Classes
  3. Student proceeds to the Accounting Office for Computation of Charges.
  4. Student goes to the Cashier’s Office & pays the required down payment.
    *FINAL ENROLMENT FORM ( FEF)
    *STUDENT ID NO.
  5. Student FILLS-UP the FINAL ENROLMENT FORM COMPLETELY. ( Please Print Legibly)
  6. Student proceeds to the Registrar’s Office for Submission of duly Accomplished FINAL ENROLMENT FORM (FEF) together with the TEMPORARY ENROLMENT FORM (TEF). Wait for the release of your STUDY LOAD...
  7. Any of the Dean/Program Head is not available during the Enrolment, Please proceed to the Registrar’s Office
  8. REMINDERS: Always bring your CURRICULUM EVALUATION EVERY ENROLMENT FOR EVALUATION… Thank you for Choosing ACI as the School for your Future
