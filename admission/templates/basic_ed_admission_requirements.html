Basic Education Admission Requirements

The following procedures shall be observed:

    Senior High School
  1. Pay Entrance Exam to the cashier.
  2. Proceed to the Library and look for the library in-charge to take Entrance Exam.
  3. Bring Entrance Exam results to the Senior High School in-charge of enrolment together with all the required documents.
  4. Fill out the Senior High School enrolment form.
  5. Proceed to cashier to pay for the Entrance Fee and School ID.
  6. Fill out the school id form from cashier.
  7. Present all the receipt acquired to the Senior High School in-charge.
    Junior High School & Elementary
  1. Proceed to cashier to pay for the Entrance Fee and School ID.
  2. Fill out the school id form from cashier.
  3. Present all the receipt acquired to the Junior High School or Elementary in-charge of enrolment.
  4. Fill out the enrolment form and let it signed by the Basic Education Principal for approval.

Requirements


    Senior High School
  • Entrance Exam Result
  • Entrance Fee
  • Original Form 138/SF9-SHS
  • Good Moral Character
  • PSA Issued Birth Certificate
  • NCAE Result
  • Passport Size Photo (1 pc.)
  • Long Brown Envelop

    • Junior High School & Elementary
  • Entrance Exam Result
  • Entrance Fee
  • Original Form 138(card)
  • Good Moral Character
  • PSA Issued Birth Certificate
  • Passport Size Photo (2 pc.)
  • 1X1 Size Photo (2 pc.)
